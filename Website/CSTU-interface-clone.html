2 Faculties

Chandpur Science and Technology University boasts two distinguished faculties. The Faculty of Engineering and Technology delivers advanced programs across engineering disciplines, promoting innovation and hands-on skills. Meanwhile, the Faculty of Business Studies offers comprehensive courses in management and analytics, preparing students for thriving careers in the corporate sphere.

3 Departments

The Faculty of Engineering and Technology includes two departments: Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in software development and computational technologies, and Information and Communication Technology, emphasizing networking and communication systems. Conversely, the Faculty of Business Studies hosts the Department of Business Administration, offering education in diverse areas of business management and administration.
